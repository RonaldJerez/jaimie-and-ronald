Gift Registry

In the Chinese wedding tradition, the newly-weds are commonly presented with monetary gifts in red envelopes or packets by family and friends. Red is the color of weddings, and these gifts are thought to bring luck and prosperity to the couple. Red is also associated with positive traits such as loyalty, honor, courage, fertility, and passion as well as positive happenings such as success, fortune and happiness.

We are asking that our guests follow in this practice. The traditional red envelopes are often found in Asian stationary stores but may not be available to everyone, so a plain red envelope works just as well.

Please avoid gifting using a white envelope as it is considered bad luck.

Of course, you should not feel obligated to give anything at all, as having you as part of our special day will be your greatest gift to us.
